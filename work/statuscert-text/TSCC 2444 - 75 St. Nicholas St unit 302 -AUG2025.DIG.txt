Legal Proceedings, Claims 
18. There are no outstanding judgments against the Corporation. 
 
19. The  Corporation  is  not a party to any proceeding  before  a  court  of  law,  an  arbitrator  or an administrative tribunal, 
except:  
 
A) Ontario  Superior  Court  of  Justice  Court  File  No.  CV-22-00681587-0000 – TSCC  2444 is  the  plaintiff.  It 
alleges  that  the  declarant, consultants  and  contractors engaged  in  the construction  of  the  building  failed 
to  properly  design,  supply  and  install  the  riser  system  providing  heating  and  cooling  to  the  units.  Some 
components  of  the  riser  system  failed  and  caused  floods  in  2021  and  2022.  This  caused  damage  to  the 
common elements and required extensive repairs to the riser system, which have been completed. TSCC 
2444  seeks  damages  of  $750,000.  The  action  is  in  the  examination  for  discovery  phase  during  which 
TSCC 2444 quantified its damages as $951,704. 
 
B) Ontario  Superior  Court  of  Justice  Court  File  No.  CV-22-00690980-0000 – TSCC  2444  is  the  defendant 
and plaintiff by counterclaim. TSCC 2444 has settled the claim against it. In its counterclaim, TSCC 2444 
seeks  damages  against two  mechanical contractors engaged  in  repairing  components  of  the  riser  system 
in  July  2022.  In  completing  the  repairs,  one  of  the  contractors  severed  a  riser  which  contained  water, 
causing  water  damage  to  the  common  elements.  TSCC  2444  seeks  damages  of  $156,472.  Although  the 
matter was mediated early, it did not settle. The parties are proceeding to examinations for discovery. 
 
  
 
C) Small  Claims  Court  File  No.  SC  24000059560000 – TSCC 2444 is defendant to a Defendant’s Claim in 
the Small Claims Court. The plaintiffs were tenants residing in units of TSCC 2444. Their property was

contractor  defendants  made  the  Defendant’s  Claim  against  TSCC  2444  seeking  contribution  and 
indemnity. TSCC 2444 has defended the action.
